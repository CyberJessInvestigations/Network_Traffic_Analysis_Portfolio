== Infected Host Filter ==

Tool Used:
Wireshark

Filter Used:
ip.addr == 10.2.28.88

Known Infected Host:
10.2.28.88

Finding:
After applying the filter, Wireshark showed only traffic involving 10.2.28.88. The filtered results confirmed that this host was communicating across multiple protocols, including DNS, Kerberos, SMB, LDAP, and HTTP-related traffic.

Observation:
Filtering on the infected host removed unrelated traffic from the PCAP and made it easier to focus on host details such as the MAC address, hostname, and Windows username.
